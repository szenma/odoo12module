User Guide

After installing the app, configure Overtime rates on the employees' 'Working Schedule' & the timezone.

There are two 'Overtime Types' varying in the method of appliance and fields added to configuration:

1-Per Request:

As the Administrator, Navigate to 'Settings' in 'Payroll' module's Configuration menu and set the 'Overtime Type' to 'Per Request' which will add 3 new fields to configure accordingly (Set the users you would like to be notified of Overtime request, activateAnalytic Entries if needed, and Set an Expense account).

A logged in User (ie: Max) can create a new 'Overtime' calculation request through 'Payroll', fill in the needed details (Date/Time, description, the related project if Analytic Entries where activated...etc) then 'Confirm' it. Notice that the Duration of the Overtime & its rate per hour are autamatically calculated and that also the Date/Time fields show the duration according to the timezone on the employee's profile.

The chain of approval starts with the requester's Direct Manager (ie: Hans) who can view all his subordinates' Overtime Requests & approve them by clicking on 'First Approval' button.


The next level of approval is by the Department Manager (ie: Ashley) , who can view & approve all the department related employees 'Overtime Requests', also clicks on 'Second Approval' button which moves the request to the last level of approval conducted by HR Department Manager (ie: Antoine) who needs to click the 'Done' button.


Once the request is Done, the Advisor can view the reflected Analytic Lines from the 'Analytic Line' field in the request itself.


2-Based on Attendance:

As the Administrator, Navigate to 'Settings' in 'Payroll' module's Configuration menu and set the 'Overtime Type' to 'Based on Attendance'.

Create a new 'Employee Payslip' from Payroll, fill in the Employee details and their Overtime calculation will be visible in a line under the 'Other Inputs' table. Notice that the hours calculated are the worked hours outside of the Employee 'Working Schedule' excluding break hours.

After computing the sheet, you will be able to see the added 'Overtime Salary Rule' in the payslip (Don't forge to first add the rule to the 'Salary Structure' from Payroll Configurations).
